HAND MASK

Chrysalis Collection
Â£70.00

Materials: Mudroc plaster, Wool, Plastic/Glass beads
Description: A plaster-casted hand using Mudroc, created into a mask using crochet. Seams are loose and weighed down with a mix of plastic/glass beads of varying colour.
About: The Chrysalis Collection is originally inspired by the process of chrysalis, usually seen in caterpillars. We wanted to find ways to mimic and create pieces that were on-brand, fashionable and embodied the theme we were inspired by. After expanding our themes and ideas for this collection, we made pieces based on the process of chrysalis, self-isolation and society during and post-COVID-19.
The 'Hand Mask' was created with Mudroc, a rough material when dry and flakey at the edges. We thought this material was perfect for the 'chrysalis' or 'cocoon' effect we were looking for.
